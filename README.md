# MyInterationApp

PROBLEM :
Cora finds its hard to maintain a social connections either because of work or just a hectic schedule.She worries she'll lose touch with others and her own life ,which leaves her guilty and disconnected.

SOLUTION :
The MyInteractionApp gives cora a tiny, time based nudge. All she has to do is to enter the time of day   (Morning,Mid-Morning,Afternoon,Mid-afternoon,Evening,Midnight) and the app returns a low-effort and simple responce. The suggestion is simple and can jump-start a social connection. This is so she does not feel  like her conversations or activities are forced.
