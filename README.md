# MyInterationApp

PROBLEM :
Cora finds its hard to maintain a social connections either because of work or just a hectic schedule.She worries she'll lose touch with others and her own life ,which leaves her guilty and disconnected.

SOLUTION :
The MyInteractionApp gives cora a tiny, time based nudge. All she has to do is to enter the time of day   (Morning,Mid-Morning,Afternoon,Mid-afternoon,Evening,Midnight) and the app returns a low-effort and simple responce. The suggestion is simple and can jump-start a social connection. This is so she does not feel  like her conversations or activities are forced.

WHY IT HELPS CORA :
.Removes decision fatigue: one input= one clear action.
.Feel natural:The suggestions are timed to real routine ,not random alerts.
.Reduces guilt: She has a visible record of outreach, easing anxiety.
.Build habit: She can gain a proper work life balance through creating these small habits and not feel overwhelmed by it.

IMPACT
Regular, effortless checks can help CORA maintain healthy lifestyle and relationships .She can feel less isolated and restart her social rhythm.The app isn't just commands but cues to jumpstart her life again.
